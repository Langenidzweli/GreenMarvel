What is the difference between your Hair Spray, Growth Oil, and Hair Food? Popular

Our three main products serve different but complementary purposes:

Vitality Hair Spray

Penetrates the scalp for maximal results, provides light hold, and fights dryness and frizz. Perfect for daily use and styling.

Growth Oil

A concentrated blend that stimulates growth, strengthens roots, and deeply nourishes hair and beard. Ideal for overnight treatment.

Hair Food

Protects your scalp and nurtures your hair with deep conditioning and intense hydration. Best for weekly deep treatments.

What is the shelf life of your products?

All Green Marvel products have a shelf life of 24 months from the manufacturing date when stored properly:

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep lids tightly closed when not in use
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Do not expose to moisture or humidity

The manufacturing date is printed on the bottom of each product.

Our products are generally safe for most users, but we recommend:

For Children (2+ years): Safe for use, but perform patch test first
For Pregnant Women: Consult with healthcare provider before use
For Sensitive Skin: Always perform patch test 24 hours before full use

If you have specific health concerns, please consult with a medical professional.
