Product Questions

What is the difference between your Hair Spray, Growth Oil, and Hair Food?

Our three main products serve different but complementary purposes:

Vitality Hair Spray - Penetrates the scalp for maximal results, provides light hold, and fights dryness and frizz.

Growth Oil - A concentrated blend that stimulates growth, strengthens roots, and deeply nourishes hair and beard.

Hair Food - Protects your scalp and nurtures your hair with deep conditioning and intense hydration.

Many customers use them together for optimal results, which is why we offer combo deals.

Which product is best for my hair type?

All our products are designed to work with all hair types, but here are some recommendations:

  • For dry, frizzy hair: Hair Food provides intense moisture and frizz control
  • For promoting growth: Growth Oil stimulates follicles and reduces breakage
  • For daily styling and scalp health: Hair Spray offers light hold while nourishing
  • For comprehensive care: Our combo deals provide complete hair care solutions
How often should I use these products?

Usage frequency depends on your hair needs:

  • Vitality Hair Spray: Use daily or as needed for hold and nourishment
  • Growth Oil: Use daily for best results, preferably before bed
  • Hair Food: Use 3-4 times a week or as a deep treatment overnight
  • Combo products: Use 3-5 times per week as part of your routine

All products can be adjusted based on your hair's response and needs.

Are your products safe for colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes! All Green Marvel products are:

  • 100% natural and synthetic chemicals-free
  • Gentle on scalp and hair
  • Safe for sensitive skin and scalps
  • Free from harsh ingredients that could strip color

In fact, our products help repair damage caused by heat styling, coloring, and environmental stressors.

Can I use these products on my beard?

Absolutely! Our Growth Oil is specifically formulated for both hair and beard care. It:

  • Promotes fuller, more defined beards
  • Conditions the skin underneath
  • Reduces itchiness and flakes
  • Softens beard hair for easier grooming

Many of our customers use our products for comprehensive hair and beard care.

Ingredients & Safety

Are your products truly 100% natural?

Yes! All Green Marvel products are:

  • 100% natural and free from synthetic chemicals
  • Made with carefully selected botanical extracts and essential oils
  • Preserved with denatured organic alcohol (a natural preservative)
  • Eco-friendly and safe for you and the environment

We're committed to providing pure, effective hair care without harsh ingredients.

What are the key ingredients in your products?

Our products feature a powerful blend of natural ingredients:

  • Essential Oils: Rosemary, Vitamin E, Grapeseed Oil, Cedarwood Oil, Lavender Oil, Castor Oil
  • Nutrient-rich Butters: Shea Butter, Sweet Almond Oil
  • Herbal Extracts: Carefully selected for hair-loving properties
  • Preservative: Denatured Organic Alcohol (natural and safe)

Each ingredient is chosen for its specific benefits to hair and scalp health.

Do your products contain any allergens?

Our products contain natural essential oils and botanical extracts. While rare, some individuals may be sensitive to certain natural ingredients. We recommend:

  • Performing a patch test before first use
  • Checking the ingredient list if you have known allergies
  • Consulting with a dermatologist if you have sensitive skin

All ingredients are clearly listed on our product pages for your reference.

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

Ordering from Green Marvel is simple:

  1. Browse our products and select the items you want
  2. Add them to your cart
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your shipping information
  5. Choose your payment method
  6. Review and confirm your order

You'll receive an order confirmation email once your purchase is complete.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery (available in select areas)

All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We can only modify or cancel orders that haven't been processed for shipping yet. Please contact us immediately if you need to change your order.

Once an order has been shipped, we cannot cancel it, but you can return it following our return policy.

Shipping & Returns

What are your shipping options and costs?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - R50
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - R100
  • Free Shipping: On orders over R300

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase:

  • Contact our customer service within 30 days of receipt
  • Return the product in its original condition
  • Once we receive the return, we'll process your refund

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we primarily ship within South Africa. However, we're working on expanding our international shipping options.

For international orders, please contact our customer service team for availability and shipping costs to your location.
