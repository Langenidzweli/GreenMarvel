Quick Questions

How long will delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is 1-2 business days. Free shipping on orders over R300.

What happens after ordering?

You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately. We process orders within 24 hours and send tracking information once shipped.

Do I receive an invoice?

Yes, you'll receive a detailed invoice via email immediately after your order is confirmed.

Is refund possible?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Products must be returned in original condition.

Delivery charges?

Standard: R50 (3-5 days), Express: R100 (1-2 days), Free shipping on orders over R300.

What is package tracking?

Track your order in real-time with the tracking number we provide once your order ships.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Contact us immediately.
